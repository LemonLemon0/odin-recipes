Pizza

Description

Pizza is an italian dish where dough is topped with sauce and cheese then baked. It has now become a worldwide dish, with many different areas putting their own unique spin on the dish. There are a variety of toppings and sizes to choose from, and you have a lot of freedom in what you can create. Read below to find out how to start making your very own pizza

Ingredients

  • Tomato puree
  • Preferred cheese
  • Pizza dough
  • Any extra toppings

Instructions

  1. Roll out pizza dough
  2. Spread tomato puree
  3. Add cheese on top
  4. Add any other toppings
  5. Place in oven
  6. Wait for it to bake
  7. Carefully take out of oven
  8. Cut into slices and serve
