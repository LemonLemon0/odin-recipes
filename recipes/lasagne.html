Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is an italian dish with several layers of meat, cheese, pasta, and sauce. It has now become a worldwide dish, iconic for its appearance in the iconic comic "Garfield". There are a variety of ingredients you can choose to add, so you can truly create your very own. Read below to find out how to start you can start making it

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Pasta sheets
  • Bolognese sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Any extra ingredients, such as vegetables

Instructions

  1. Add meat to pan
  2. Cook until brown
  3. Spread an even layer of bolognese sauce over dish
  4. Place single layer of pasta sheets on top
  5. Spread a layer of béchamel sauce
  6. Repeat last 3 steps as many times as necessar
  7. Place into oven
  8. Wait for it to bake
  9. Carefully take out of oven
  10. Cut into slices and serve
