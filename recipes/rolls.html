Cinnamon Rolls

Description

Cinnamon rolls are a swedish dish where dough is filled with cinnamon, rolled, and then drizzled with glazing. It has now become a worldwide dish, traditionally served for breakfast, despite it's sugary contents. This is a simple, yet comforting dish that isn't too complex to create. Read below to find out how to start making your very own cinnamon rolls

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cinnamon
  • White and brown sugar
  • Cream cheese frosting

Instructions

  1. Roll out dough
  2. Mix cinnamon and sugars
  3. Spread thin layer over dough
  4. roll dough over to create a tube
  5. Cut into rolls
  6. Place onto tray and into oven
  7. Wait for them to bake
  8. Carefully take out of oven
  9. Drizzle with frosting and serve
